SERVICES

At Carolina's Insulations, we offer a wide range of Insulations services. Our team delivers top-notch insulation solutions to your home or business.

Fiberglass Insulation

  • Traditional pink or yellow batt insulation for walls, attics, & floors.
  • Cost effective
  • Fire resistant, and easy to install

Blow-In Isulation

  • Loose-fill insulation blown into attics and walls for seamless coverage.
  • Fills gaps
  • Energy efficient.

Crawlspace insulation

  • Specialized insulation that prevents moisture
  • Reduce energy loss
  • Protects against mold & structural damage

Crawlspace Encapsulation

  • Complete crawlspace sealing with vapour barriers.
  • Moisture and pest protection
  • Improves quality of inddor air.

Air sealing

  • Professional sealing of gaps and cracks to prevent air leakage.
  • Reduced enegy bills and eliminate drafts

Vapor Barrier

  • Moisture-resistant barrier that prevents water vapor penetration.
  • Essential protection against mold
  • Protection against rot.

Insulation Removal

  • Safe removal of old, damaged or contaminated insulation.
  • Clears the way for new, safe and efficient materials.
